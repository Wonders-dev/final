  The multi-page website is to showcase my crochet business and the goods being sold. 
On the home page, there are two buttons, “View what’s for sale!” and “About the company!” You click the “View what’s for sale!” button and it leads you to a page where you can look through what is being sold!
Each good has a respective button on the website page that can be tapped to take you to the good’s information. At the top left, there is a home button that you can click to get back to the home page. 
The next button you click on the home page is  “About the company!” and it takes you to a page explaining the company and why it was created! You can watch an informational video, and then leave a comment of your opinion at the bottom. 
There is also a home button to take you back to the home page here too.

  I like how my website turned out, but if I were to actually use it for my business I would add a few things.
I would make it so that the comments can be stored and viewed by other people strolling through the website.
I would also make it so that there is some way to actually buy and pay for a crocheted good. 
Another thing I would look deeper into was the safety of the website, as I do not want my consumers to be unsafe because my website is easy to hack into.
